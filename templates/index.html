Our Story
WHERE NAIROBI
COMES
TO POUR
The Mad Monkey started as a simple idea: Nairobi deserved a bottle shop that felt as alive as the city itself. No stuffy wine walls, no hushed atmospheres. Just great drinks, honest prices, and a space that brings people together.
Today we're a retail shop, a bar and lounge, and a delivery service rolled into one. From the neighbourhood regular to the uptown crowd — everyone is welcome at The Mad Monkey.
